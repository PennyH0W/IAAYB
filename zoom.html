About the Class

What You’ll Need

To fully enjoy the class, make sure you're dressed in comfortable clothing suitable for dancing.
Ensure you have a stable internet connection and enough space to move around freely.
Additionally, you might want to use a scarf or hip belt to enhance your movements, although this is optional.

Tips for a Great Experience

To get the most out of your class, be sure to join the Zoom meeting a few minutes early to settle in.
It's a good idea to mute your microphone when not speaking to minimize background noise.
Adjust your camera so that Janet can clearly see your movements.
Most importantly, have fun and don’t hesitate to ask questions!
