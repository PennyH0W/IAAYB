Overview

Welcome to a world where passion meets resilience. Janet Sherfey, a multi-talented instructor, author, and creative force, invites you to explore the art of belly dance through her unique lens. With a rich background that spans from accounting to tattoo artistry, and a life story marked by incredible recovery and transformation, Janet embodies the spirit of eclectic adventure. Her latest endeavor, It's All About Your Belly, is more than just a book—it's a testament to the healing power of dance and the vibrant journey of life itself. Join Janet as she shares her love for belly dance, her teaching philosophy, and the joy she finds in every rhythm and movement.

About the Instructor

With a master’s degree in accounting, Janet’s penchant for bean counting has led her everywhere from the world’s largest defense contractor, to a seafood processing ship on Kodiak Island, Alaska.  Owning an ever-present flair for the eclectic, Janet has taken sojourns into many occupations from driving a team of draft horses, to tattoo artistry.  Along with achieving several Fitness Instructor certificates such as Spinning, Zumba, Hooping and Belly Dance (her true love) she is the purveyor of “One of a Kind Costumes” where she creates Fantasy wear and accessories for various events across the country. She’s currently working on another book compiling real stories from real people and their memorable tales from their online dating experiences. Accountant, poster girl, belly dance fitness instructor, costume designer, miniature goat wrangler, and now author, Janet’s life is a roller coaster of passions. Come join the ride!

~ Janet Sherfey, Author of It's All About Your Belly

Janet's Journey

Janet is a passionate belly dance instructor who has dedicated her life to teaching and inspiring others through the art of belly dance. After a life-changing car accident left her wheelchair and walker-bound, belly dancing became her pathway to recovery. The exercise, endurance, and motivation she gained through belly dance helped her overcome numerous fractures and a heart contusion. This transformative experience inspired Janet to write her book, It's All About Your Belly, and she has since appeared on several TV shows across the US to share her story and the healing power of dance.

Teaching Philosophy

Janet's main passion lies in teaching belly dance, and her classes cover a wide range of styles including Greek, Egyptian, SharQui, and various fusion forms that blend belly dance with country, hip-hop, burlesque, hula, Tahitian, and salsa. In addition to belly dancing, Janet is a certified Zumba and hula hoop instructor. She integrates yoga and stretching into her classes to enhance flexibility, prevent injuries, and ensure her students can fully enjoy the dance experience.

Accessories & Music

Janet's classes often feature the use of traditional dance accessories such as Isis Wings, veils, fan veils, zills, and Tahitian I'i's. The music accompanying her classes is as diverse as her dance styles, ranging from classical belly dancing music to Greek, Indian, country, hip-hop, reggae, salsa, Latin, folkloric, and beyond.
